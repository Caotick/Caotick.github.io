About The Beginning After The End Manga

“The Beginning After The End” is a manga based on a popular web novel. It revolves around King Grey, who has unrivaled strength, wealth, and influence in a world governed by martial ability. However, such an external success hides an empty life devoid of joy and fulfillment. After his untimely and mysterious death, he is reborn in a magical new world as Arthur Leywin, where he has the opportunity to live a second life with a fresh beginning.

In his new life, Arthur is blessed with a loving family and an inborn talent for magic, contrasting sharply with his previous life. This new world, however, is full of challenges and dangers, including monstrous creatures and complex magical abilities. The story follows Arthur’s growth from childhood, navigating through political, social, and magical challenges in this new realm.

Throughout the series, themes of growth, salvation, and the desire to change one’s fate are prominent. Arthur’s journey is not just about physical growth and learning magic but also about understanding the importance of connections, love, and the complexities of human nature. This manga offers a blend of action, adventure, and fantasy, appealing to readers who enjoy delicate character development and expansive world-building.
