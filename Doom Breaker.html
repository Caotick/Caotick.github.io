Reincarnation of the Suicidal Battle God

Reincarnation of the Suicidal Battle God Manga is a korean manga written by Cheong Dam. Reincarnation of the suicidal battle god manga is also called To a New Life and the korean name is 투신전생기. The anime of reincarnation of the suicidal battle god was released first on June 27, 2021 by Naver Webtoon, and then the english translation published on August 6, 2021. The main charaters of reincarnation of the suicidal battle god manga are : Zephyr, Altair Justina Cayenne, Alfonso Giornetti, Anna Primrose, Lucius Giornetti and others. The manga’s main genres are : Action, Adventure andFantasy.

Description

Reincarnation of the suicidal battle god manga is a story in which the protagonist, who died after a fight with a demon, returns to 10 years ago with memories and various buffs. Not only the demon gods but the gods also overlook the slaughter of humans and view the destruction of mankind as one of the entertainments, so it is expected that the main character will take revenge on the gods in some way.
